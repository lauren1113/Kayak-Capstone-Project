How to Hold Your Paddle

First, make sure the blades of the paddle are aligned and facing the same direction. Then, grasp your paddle with both hands, with your knuckles over the top of the paddle. You want to make sure your hands are evenly spaced apart. It helps to hold the paddle over your head, aligning the center of the paddle over the top of your head. Your hands are aligned correctly when your elbows are at a 90-degree angle. Once you've found this position, lower your paddle in front of you. The longer edge of each blade should be on top, and the scooped sides of the paddle blades should be facing you.
